# Ripple Cross-Border Payments Projects
## 1. Cross-Border Payment Cost Analysis (SWIFT vs XRP)
Conducted an Excel-based financial analysis comparing traditional SWIFT transfers with an XRP-based settlement model across 10 currencies to evaluate cost, FX efficiency, and settlement speed.
### 🔗 Links
- [Download Excel Model](https://github.com/masonkubenez/cross-border-payment-analysis/raw/main/XRP_Transfer_Comparison_10_Currencies.xlsx)
###  Key Results
- XRP reduced transaction fees by **90–94%**
- FX rate improvements added **~0.7%–2.6% additional value**
- Total transfer savings averaged **~3.3%–5.0%**
- Settlement time improved from **2–5 days → near real-time**
### 💡 Key Insight
XRP-based transfers consistently reduced both cost and settlement time, demonstrating the efficiency of blockchain-based liquidity solutions compared to traditional intermediary banking systems.

## 2. Ripple Cross-Border Payments Research Paper
Analyzed Ripple’s role in modernizing global payment infrastructure, focusing on how XRP-based liquidity reduces reliance on pre-funded accounts, lowers transaction costs, and improves settlement efficiency.
### 🔗 Links
- 📄 [View Research Paper](https://github.com/masonkubenez/cross-border-payment-analysis/raw/main/Ripple-Cross-Border-Payments-Analysis-Paper.docx)
###  Topics Covered
- How SWIFT works
- Limitations of intermediary banking
- RippleNet & XRP liquidity model
- Cost and speed comparison
- Real-world use cases
###  Key Insight
Ripple’s technology demonstrates how blockchain-based payment infrastructure can significantly improve international money movement by reducing delays, lowering costs, and increasing capital efficiency.
